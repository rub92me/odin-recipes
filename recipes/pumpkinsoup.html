Pumpkin Soup

Recipe Overview

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin – Peeled and chopped into large chunks
  • Onion and garlic
  • Stock/broth and water
  • Milk, cream or half and half
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place peeled pumpkin, onion, garlic, broth/stock and water into a pot
  2. Boil rapidly for 15 minutes until pumpkin is very tender
  3. Use a stick blender or transfer to blender to blitz smooth
  4. Adjust salt and pepper to taste, then add either milk OR cream – whichever you prefer
  5. Serve hot or cold with bread or by itself
