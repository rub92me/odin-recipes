Pizza Picante

Recipe Overview

Ingredients

  • Pizza base - buy from Coles or Woolies
  • 75 grams of grated cheese
  • Tomato paste
  • 10 Olives
  • 10 Jalapeno slices
  • 50 grams of thinly sliced salami

Steps

  1. Remove plastic and carton packaging from base
  2. Add extra tomato paste to taste (optional)
  3. Sprinkle grated cheese on base to cover entire surface
  4. Chop olives and jalapenos
  5. Cover base with all the toppings, nicely distributed
  6. Place in preheated oven (220%C) for 12-15 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and serve
