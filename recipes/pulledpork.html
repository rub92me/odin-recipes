Pulled Pork

Recipe Overview

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 small yellow onion, peeled and cut
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 (4-pound) pork shoulder

Steps

  1. Place the broth, apple cider vinegar, jalapeño, and onion in the bottom of your slow cooker in an even layer
  2. Combine the brown sugar, pepper, salt, paprika, and garlic powder in a small bowl to make the dry rub
  3. Cover and cook on low until it easily pulls apart when you pierce it with a fork and twist, 8 to 9 hours
  4. Move the pork from the slow cooker to a platter and let rest for 20 minutes
  5. Remove any large fatty pieces and discard, if desired, then use two forks to shred the pork
  6. Add some of the juices, jalapeño, and onion to keep moist
  7. Serve with coleslaw on bread or by itself
